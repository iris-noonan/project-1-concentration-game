Concentration matching game

Intructions: Start the game by pressing play. The timer will start. Select a card to turn and show a Studio Ghibli film. Select another card to try to find the matching film. If it's a match you win, if not the card will return to the original form until you find two matching films. Continue to play until you match all the films and all the cards are revealed. Try to complete this in less than 2min 30s and less than 50 moves!
